# Baxandall-Audio-Tone-Control
Design and implementation of a Baxandall audio tone control circuit with adjustable bass and treble response. Includes LTspice simulation, parameter sweeps, and frequency response analysis.

# Baxandall Audio Tone Control

## Project Objective
Design and simulate a Baxandall audio tone control circuit capable of independently adjusting bass and treble frequencies.

## Methodology

### Step 1: Frequency Selective Networks
Studied and simulated:
- Low-pass filter behavior
- High-pass filter behavior
- Effect of RC values on cutoff frequency

### Step 2: Potentiometer Network Design
Investigated how variable resistance affects signal paths and frequency response.

### Step 3: Baxandall Circuit Design
Combined bass and treble control sections into a complete tone control network.

### Step 4: Simulation and Optimization
Performed LTspice simulations and parameter sweeps to analyze:
- Bass boost/cut
- Treble boost/cut
- Overall frequency response
